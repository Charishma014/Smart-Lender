# Empathy Map

## Persona 1: The Loan Applicant
- **Thinks & Feels:** Anxious about approval, confused by financial jargon, wants a quick answer.
- **Hears:** "It takes 2-3 weeks for processing."
- **Sees:** Complex paper forms, long bank queues.
- **Says & Does:** Searches online for quick loans, tries to calculate eligibility manually.
- **Pains:** Rejection without explanation, waiting time.
- **Gains:** Instant approval, clear reasons for decisions, easy tracking.

## Persona 2: The Loan Officer
- **Thinks & Feels:** Overwhelmed by paperwork, worried about risk assessment.
- **Hears:** Complaints about slow processing.
- **Pains:** Manual data entry, subjective decision making.
- **Gains:** Automated pre-screening, data-backed insights, streamlined workflow.

# Problem Statement

**The Problem:** Traditional loan approval processes are slow, manual, and prone to human bias. Applicants have to wait weeks to know their eligibility, and there is a lack of transparency in why a loan is approved or rejected.

**The Impact:** Customers face delays and frustration, while financial institutions spend excessive man-hours evaluating applications.

**The Goal:** To create "SmartLender", an AI-powered loan eligibility platform that provides instant, objective, and accurate loan predictions using machine learning, along with a user-friendly dashboard to track applications.

# Team Involvement in Demonstration

- **Lead Developer:** Demonstrates the application flow, technical architecture, and answers backend/ML questions.
- **Data Scientist:** Explains the dataset, EDA, feature engineering, and the choice of XGBoost.
- **UI/UX Designer:** Explains the design choices, empathy map, and customer journey.
